Component 1

The Self-Healing
Scraping Agent

Traditional scrapers break the moment a website changes its HTML structure. Our agent used LLM-driven navigation — instead of hard-coded selectors, the agent reads the page, reasons about its structure, and adapts its strategy on the fly.

Built with Google ADK for agent orchestration, Playwright for browser control, and Firecrawl for structured extraction. The agent monitors 3 competitor websites and flags changes automatically.

01
Navigate. Playwright opens the target page. The agent receives a snapshot of the rendered HTML.
02
Reason. The LLM analyzes the structure and identifies the relevant data — without pre-defined selectors.
03
Extract. Firecrawl pulls structured data based on the agent's understanding. If the page changed, the reasoning step adapts.
04
Flag. New or changed data is flagged and surfaced. The agent logs what changed and when — no manual review required to notice a shift.
Component 2

Competitive Intelligence
Pipeline

Beyond structured data, the second component ingests unstructured signals from blogs, news sources, and social media. The pipeline uses entity recognition to extract relevant brand mentions, product names, and market events, topic analysis to cluster signals by theme, and noise filtering to separate signal from volume.

The output surfaces emerging market signals — trends, competitor launches, sentiment shifts — before they become obvious. Powered by Vertex AI on Google Cloud.

🏷️
Entity Recognition
Extracts brand mentions, product names, people, and market events from raw unstructured text across news, blogs, and social feeds.
📊
Topic Analysis
Clusters signals into themes — a product launch, a PR controversy, a pricing shift — so analysts see patterns, not a raw feed.
🔇
Noise Filtering
High-volume sources produce a lot of irrelevant content. The pipeline filters by relevance score before surfacing anything to the analyst.
⚡
Near Real-Time
Signals are ingested and processed continuously — not in daily batch jobs. Market intelligence that was available in days is now available in minutes.
